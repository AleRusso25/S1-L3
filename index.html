Focaccia with caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onion, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
Fot the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • balsamic vinegar1 tablespoon
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oli, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-size mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absored. You don't need to actually knead the dought, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand incorporate the last of flour...
